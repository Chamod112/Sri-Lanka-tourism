About Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka, known as the "Pearl of the Indian Ocean," is a beautiful island nation located in South Asia. It boasts rich cultural heritage, stunning natural landscapes, and diverse wildlife.

Culture

The island has a mix of Buddhist, Hindu, Muslim, and Christian traditions, reflected in its festivals, food, and architecture.

History

With a history dating back thousands of years, Sri Lanka has been influenced by various civilizations, including ancient kingdoms, colonial powers, and modern developments.

Food

Sri Lankan cuisine features spicy curries, rice dishes, and fresh seafood, with unique flavors and aromatic spices.
