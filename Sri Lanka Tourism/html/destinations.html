Top Destinations in Sri Lanka

Colombo

The bustling capital city, rich in history, shopping, and nightlife.

Kandy

A scenic city with the famous Temple of the Tooth and beautiful lakes.

Galle

A charming coastal city famous for its Dutch Fort and beaches.

Nuwara Eliya

Known as "Little England," famous for tea plantations and cool climate.

Jaffna

A cultural and historical city with unique Tamil heritage and temples.
